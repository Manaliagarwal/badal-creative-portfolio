Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer professional video editing, cinematography, podcast production, color grading, sound design, and social media content creation. Each project is tailored to your specific needs and goals.

What's your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies based on project complexity. Simple edits: 2-3 days, Standard projects: 5-7 days, Complex productions: 10-14 days. Rush delivery available with additional fee.

Do you work remotely or locally?

I work both remotely and locally. Based in Pune, India, I'm available for on-location shoots in Pune and surrounding areas. Remote editing and post-production services available worldwide.

What's your pricing structure?

Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. I offer competitive rates with flexible packages. Contact me for a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements and budget.

How many revisions do you offer?

Standard packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions available at a nominal fee. My goal is to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.
